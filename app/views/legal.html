Personal information :

As used herein, the term "personal information" is defined as any information that identifies the user. This information may also be used in some circumstances to create aggregate information for the overall users of the app.

Navimate App collects sensitive information such as user's name, phone number, location, phone contacts and any other custom information that is asked while using the app. To enhance user experience, we also collect your device information such as model nuber, android version etc... This helps us streamline our services for all devices and provide you a better experience.

Collection :

Navimate App collects information through different ways.

  • user may prompt an action in the software (such as sharing location)
  • user may respond to software prompts (such as location requests)
  • user may enable services to run in background (such as sharing tracking)

For complete transparency, Navimate App shows a permanent notification to the user whenever his/her location is being shared.

In case of an app crash, some device information may get transferred to a secure database for later analysis. This helps us catch bugs in our software. We do not share this information with any entity and only use it for software enhancement.

Storage :

Navimate maintains high quality and secure servers on certified third party cloud platforms. User's personal information may be stored temporarily or permanently on this server.

If the user has registered with his phone number on the navimate app, his phone number is stored on the server. Uninstalling the app may not necessarily remove this data. If you have any complaints if Navimate and would like to remove your information permanently, you may contact us. We will provide you a valid timeframe.

User's location is stored on the server only temporarily for the duration of tracking.
